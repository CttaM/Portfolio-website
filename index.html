About

I'm a fun, creative and enthusiastic designer. I enjoy taking on challenges and finding creative ways to solve them. Creating meaningful and seemless exeriences is what I strive for and I always take pride in the work that I do.

I like to focus on user research, and the process that it follows. From conducting interviews, to then developing high fidelity prototypes mainly with Figma. I am also familier with HTML, CSS and JavaScript and can do some front end development.
